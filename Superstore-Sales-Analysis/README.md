# Superstore Sales Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from a Superstore dataset to identify key trends, profitable categories, and business opportunities.

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Cleaning

## 🔍 Business Questions

* Which category generates the most profit?
* Which region performs best?
* What are the sales trends over time?
* Which products are loss-making?

## 📈 Key Insights

📈 Revenue & Profit
Total sales reached $2.29M, generating $286K profit (~12.5% margin)
Profit is significantly lower than sales → indicates margin pressure
🏆 Category Performance
Technology is the highest revenue and profit-generating category
Office Supplies contributes steady revenue with moderate profit
Furniture shows weak profitability, indicating potential losses or high discounts

👉 Insight:

Business should prioritize Technology and optimize pricing/discounts in Furniture

🌍 Regional Performance
West region is the top-performing region in both sales and profit
Other regions (Central, South, East) contribute significantly less

👉 Insight:

Growth opportunities exist in underperforming regions

📅 Sales Trend
Sales show inconsistent monthly performance with fluctuations
A sharp spike in the final period suggests:
seasonal demand OR
bulk/large orders

👉 Insight:

Sales are not stable → need better demand forecasting

⚠️ Profitability Concern
Some categories/sub-categories generate high sales but low profit

👉 Insight:

Discounts or costs are reducing profitability → requires optimization
